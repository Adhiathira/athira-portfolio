Comprehensive Legal Services Across Every Practice Area

Whether you are an individual facing a civil or family matter, a business navigating regulatory complexities, or an NRI dealing with property or succession issues in India — we have the expertise to represent you before the High Court of Kerala, district courts, family courts, and tribunals across the country.

What We Handle

Our practice spans the full spectrum of civil, criminal, commercial, and regulatory law — built on a foundation of thorough legal research, strategic advocacy, and regular appearance before Kerala's highest courts.

Constitutional and Writ Petition Litigation

Individuals Businesses

High Court of Kerala

We file and argue writ petitions before the High Court of Kerala on matters involving fundamental rights violations, unlawful state action, administrative overreach, and regulatory challenges that require intervention at the constitutional level.

  • Constitution of India — Articles 226 and 227

Civil and Property Law

Individuals Businesses NRIs

From title verification and injunction suits to land boundary disputes and recovery of dues, we handle the full range of civil litigation and property transactions. We advise on documentation, enforce contractual rights, and secure possession and title through the civil courts.

  • Code of Civil Procedure 1908
  • Indian Contract Act 1872
  • Transfer of Property Act 1882
  • Specific Relief Act 1963
  • Registration Act 1908
  • Indian Easements Act 1882
  • Limitation Act 1963
  • Title verification
  • Injunction suits
  • Recovery suits
  • Land and boundary disputes
  • Property documentation and registration

Criminal Law

Individuals

We represent accused persons, complainants, and victims across the full arc of criminal proceedings — from securing bail at the earliest stage to defending or prosecuting trials, pursuing appeals, and filing quashing petitions before the High Court of Kerala.

  • Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita 2023
  • Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita 2023
  • Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam 2023
  • NDPS Act 1985
  • Prevention of Corruption Act 1988
  • Bail applications
  • Criminal trials
  • Criminal appeals
  • Quashing petitions

Family and Matrimonial Law

Individuals NRIs

Family disputes require both legal precision and sensitivity. We handle divorce petitions, maintenance claims, child custody and guardianship proceedings, domestic violence cases, and matrimonial property disputes before family courts and the High Court of Kerala.

  • Family Courts Act 1984
  • Hindu Marriage Act 1955
  • Special Marriage Act 1954
  • Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act 2005
  • Dowry Prohibition Act 1961
  • Divorce
  • Maintenance and alimony
  • Child custody and guardianship
  • Domestic violence remedies
  • Matrimonial property disputes

Consumer Protection and Insurance Law

Individuals Businesses

When a product fails, a service falls short, or an insurance claim is wrongfully denied, you have a legal remedy. We represent clients before consumer disputes redressal commissions at the district, state, and national levels, and before insurance dispute forums.

  • Consumer Protection Act 2019
  • Insurance Act 1938
  • IRDAI Regulations
  • Consumer commission representation
  • Insurance dispute forum proceedings
  • Deficiency of service claims
  • Unfair trade practice complaints

Corporate, Financial, and Commercial Law

Businesses

National Company Law Tribunal, Debt Recovery Tribunal

We advise and represent businesses, financial institutions, and creditors in corporate disputes, loan recovery proceedings, insolvency matters, and regulatory enforcement actions. Our practice covers the full commercial litigation and advisory spectrum.

  • Companies Act 2013
  • Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code 2016
  • SARFAESI Act 2002
  • Financial disputes
  • Loan recovery (DRT proceedings)
  • Regulatory proceedings
  • Corporate advisory

Real Estate and Land Regulation

Individuals Businesses NRIs

Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA), Kerala

We advise and represent clients in RERA complaints against builders and developers, and handle matters arising under Kerala's land laws and property registration statutes — protecting buyers, owners, and investors in real estate disputes.

  • Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act 2016
  • Kerala land laws and property registration statutes
  • RERA complaints against builders
  • Developer disputes
  • Kerala land law matters
  • Property registration advisory

Technology, E-Commerce, and Data Protection

Businesses

As digital commerce and data regulation expand, legal compliance in this domain has become critical. We advise businesses on obligations under the IT Act and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, and handle matters arising from online fraud, cybercrime, and e-commerce disputes.

  • Information Technology Act 2000
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023
  • IT Act advisory and compliance
  • DPDPA advisory for businesses
  • E-commerce legal matters
  • Cybercrime complaints

Arbitration and Alternative Dispute Resolution

Individuals Businesses

Arbitral Tribunals and mediation forums

Not every dispute needs to go to court. We represent clients in arbitration proceedings and other ADR mechanisms — providing the same strategic rigour as litigation advocacy while pursuing faster, confidential resolution of commercial and civil disputes.

  • Arbitration and Conciliation Act 1996
  • Arbitration proceedings
  • Mediation support
  • Arbitration clause drafting
  • Enforcement of arbitral awards

NRI Legal Services

NRIs

For overseas Indians, managing legal matters in India without being physically present is a serious challenge. We provide dedicated NRI legal services covering matrimonial disputes, property and land matters, succession and inheritance proceedings, and general court representation — without requiring you to travel to India for most matters.

  • Matrimonial disputes and divorce proceedings
  • Property disputes and land documentation
  • Succession and inheritance matters
  • Court representation in your absence
  • Initial consultation by phone or video call

How We Work With You

Every matter begins with a conversation. Here is how we move from your first contact to active representation.

01

Tell Us What You Are Dealing With

Reach out by phone, WhatsApp, or email and let us know the broad nature of your matter. We will confirm whether it falls within our practice and whether we are the right fit — with no obligation on either side at this stage.

02

We Assess Your Legal Position

Once we understand your situation, we provide initial legal direction: what your options are, which statutes and forums apply, and what an effective strategy might look like. If documents are relevant, we will ask for them at this stage.

03

We Formalise the Engagement

If you wish to proceed, we confirm the scope of representation, fee structure, and next steps. For NRI clients or those unable to attend in person, consultations take place by phone or video call — and in most matters, your physical presence in India is not required.

04

We Build and Execute Your Legal Strategy

Every matter is approached with thorough statute research and strategic planning. We handle all filings, appearances, correspondence, and negotiations — keeping you informed at every significant stage so you always know where your matter stands.
