NRI Legal Services

Legal Representation in India — Without Requiring You to Travel

We provide dedicated legal services for Non-Resident Indians dealing with legal matters in India. Whether your dispute involves property, matrimonial issues, or inheritance, our firm handles court representation so you do not need to be physically present for proceedings.

  • Matrimonial Disputes & Divorce Proceedings Representation in divorce, maintenance, and custody matters before Kerala Family Courts — managed on your behalf from abroad.
  • Property Disputes & Land Documentation Title verification, injunction suits, partition cases, and property documentation handled without requiring your presence at each stage.
  • Succession & Inheritance Issues Succession proceedings, property partition, and inheritance dispute resolution for NRI families with assets in Kerala.
  • Court Representation Without Travel Full legal representation before courts and tribunals across India. Initial consultation by phone; further communication by video call or in person as required.

Initial legal direction and advice are provided by phone. A formal appointment is scheduled if legal assistance is warranted after the initial conversation. Video calls are available as required.
